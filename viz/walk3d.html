The construction, in 3D

The record run's actual coordinates. Press play round to watch one generation: every point flies to its enlarged-and-twisted position (blue anchors — safe by inheritance), then the green stitch points are born to reconnect them. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom. All 6 levels of the record construction, staged in the real solar system at true scale — 1 grid unit = 1 Earth radius (6,371 km), so each hop is 1–3½ Earth radii. The Moon sits 60 units out; the Sun is 109 units wide, 23,481 away. The finale spans ≈½ AU.
